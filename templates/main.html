ns a news summerizer


FAQ:

What is this?

An automatic news summary generator

How does it work?

Using Google news api, we get a list of urls of multiple news sources. We then extract the article text from the webpages. SumBasic (Nenkova and Vanderwende 2005) algorithm is used to generate a summary.

There are bugs!

I had to implement SubBasic for my NLP course assignment, I then built this just for fun. Please feel free to submit pull requests!
